Dirt Block

Dirt is a block found abundantly in most biomes under a layer of grass blocks at the top of the Overworld.

Caracteristics

  • Stackable: Yes (64)
  • Blast resistance: 0.5
  • Hardness: 0.5

Coal Block

Blocks of coal can be used as fuel in a furnace. One block of coal lasts 800 seconds (16000 ticks), which smelts 80 items.

Caracteristics

  • Stackable: Yes (64)
  • Blast resistance: 6
  • Hardness: 5
